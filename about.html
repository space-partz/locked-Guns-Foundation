About Us

We Are

The Locked Guns Foundation is a dedicated family committed to fostering a community of opportunity and security against gun violence. Rooted in our belief in responsible gun access, we strive to raise awareness and provide tangible solutions that prioritize the safety of children and families impacted by gun violence. Our mission is driven by a deep sense of responsibility to our community and a passion for protecting the lives of the most vulnerable. We have developed a tamper-actuated fluid release firearm lock—a groundbreaking invention designed to prevent unauthorized access to firearms. This innovative solution is at the heart of our foundation's efforts to reduce the tragic consequences of gun violence, particularly the loss of young lives.

We Will

Our mission to promote:

Education & Awareness: We work tirelessly to educate the public about the importance of responsible gun ownership and the life-saving potential of advanced firearm safety technology. Through community outreach, educational programs, and advocacy efforts, we aim to raise awareness and drive meaningful change.


Innovative Solutions:Our tamper-actuated fluid release firearm lock represents a significant advancement in gun safety. This device is designed to prevent unauthorized access, making it a crucial tool in reducing accidental shootings and intentional misuse of firearms. We are committed to furthering the development and adoption of this technology to protect lives and promote responsible gun ownership.

Our Vision

We envision a world where firearm safety is paramount, and where every family can live without the fear of gun-related tragedies. By advocating for responsible gun ownership and supporting the development of advanced safety mechanisms, we aim to create a safer environment for all.


Our Commitment

We are committed to growing a supportive and engaged community through our Discord server and other platforms, where we bring together individuals who share our vision of a safer world. Through this community, we aim to foster dialogue, share resources, and support those affected by gun violence.


The Locked Guns Foundation stands as a beacon of hope and action in the fight against gun violence. We are not just advocates—we are innovators, educators, and members of a family deeply invested in the safety and well-being of our community.


Join us in our mission to make a difference. Together, we can create a safer future for all.
