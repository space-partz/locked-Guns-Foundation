WHO WE ARE

A Foundation for Community Outreach

At the heart of the Locked Guns Foundation lies a steadfast commitment to preventing unauthorized access to firearms and reducing gun-related accidents. We will provide financial, emotional, and educational support to families and individuals affected by gun violence. This may include counseling services, scholarships for education, and direct financial assistance.

ABOUT US

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to protect lives through innovative firearm safety solutions and to promote a culture of responsibility among gun owners across the nation. Our vision is a world where every gun owner is empowered with the knowledge and tools necessary to secure their firearms effectively.

RECENT CAUSES

Help Us Make a Difference

By establishing outreach programs in communities affected by gun violence, partnering with local educational institutions, and supporting victims through our foundation, we will not only enhance firearm safety but also contribute to the long-term prosperity and security of at risk communities.
